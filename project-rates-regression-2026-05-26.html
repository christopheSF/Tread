P1 · Bug Confirmed
Copy & Edit inflates rate card count — 8 shown, 6 expected (project)
When copying a project (PROJECT-A → PROJECT-B), the Copy & Edit form renders 8 rate cards instead of the expected 6. Two extra items appear: ADD-ON $1/Load is listed as a fake SRS entry at position #1, and SRS-7 (order-scoped, not on the project) leaks in at position #7. The bug does not accumulate per copy hop — ORDER-C also shows 8, not 9.
Affects: R-P7.5 · R-P7.16 · R-P7.23 · R-P7.34 · R-P7.42
P1 · Bug Confirmed
Copy & Edit inflates rate card count — 8 shown, 7 expected (order)
When copying an order (ORDER-A → ORDER-B or ORDER-B → ORDER-C), the Copy & Edit form renders 8 rate cards instead of the expected 7 (6 project-inherited + 1 order-scoped SRS-7). The extra item is ADD-ON $1/Load appearing as rate card #1. All 7 legitimate rates are present — the count is inflated by the add-on surfacing as a rate entry.
Affects: R-P7.23 · R-P7.34 · R-P7.42
P2 · Missing Feature
Reports do not expose rate card data
The Projects & Orders report and order-level reports contain no rate card columns (no Customer Rate, Vendor Rate, matched SRS name, or add-on breakdown). Rate data is invisible to operators running financial reports. Tracked under REP-233 (project) and REP-234 (order).

P2 · Bug
Legacy flat rates not surfacing in new Freight Rates form
Projects and orders with a pre-migration single flat rate (no SRS) do not display that rate in the new Freight Rates UI. The rate exists in the DB but is invisible to operators — they cannot see or edit it from the new form without migrating the record first. Tracked under TRE-14596.
TRE-14596
P2 · Missing Behaviour
Best-matched rate does not update live on condition change
When a dispatcher changes the Pickup Site, Dropoff Site, Equipment Type, or Material on an active order, the system does not immediately recalculate and display the new best-matched rate. Operators must save and reload to see the updated match. Tracked under TRE-14595.
