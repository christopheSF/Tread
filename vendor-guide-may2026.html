Chapter 1 · Driver App

The driver app, step by step.

The six things every driver needs to know on day one.

Driver Training

Six steps. From app open to load delivered.

Everything a new driver needs to know, end to end.

01

Download the app

Available on App Store and Google Play, or go directly to link.tread.io/app from any phone.

02

Sign in with phone number

Drivers sign in with their phone number, then enter a one-time code sent by text. No password required.

Flow
  1. 1Enter phone number
  2. 2Receive text verification code
  3. 3Enter code → Signed in
03

Enable location and GPS

Tread tracks driver location only while a job is active. No tracking outside active jobs.

  • iOS: Allow location access when prompted
  • Android: Grant location permissions
04

Accept and start a job

The driver accepts the assigned job right from their cab and taps Start to begin.

Steps
  1. 1Open the Assigned menu to see assignments
  2. 2Tap VIEW REQUEST on a job
  3. 3Tap ACCEPT
  4. 4Tap START to begin the job
05

Add a scale ticket

Snap a photo of the paper ticket or enter the numbers manually. Tread keeps it with the job automatically.

Options
  1. 1Tap Open ticket entry
  2. 2Photo the ticket or type in details
  3. 3Ticket saved automatically
06

Stop the job — load delivered

When the load is delivered, the driver taps Stop. The job closes and the office sees it immediately.

Steps
  1. 1Tap Stop job
  2. 2Job marked Complete — visible to dispatcher
07

Add hourly timesheets Coming Soon

For hourly work, drivers log time directly in the app. No more paper timesheets to chase down.

🌱
Rolling out by region

Timesheet entry is rolling out by region. Contact your CSM to confirm availability in your area.

Chapter 2 · Mobile App

Set up your team on mobile.

Add drivers, trucks, and other users from your phone.

Mobile · Vendor Admin

Download, sign in, and add your team

Everything you need to get your account set up from your phone — before you ever open a laptop.

Download the app and sign in

  1. 1Download Tread Material Logistics from the App Store or Google Play
  2. 2Or go to link.tread.io/app on your phone
  3. 3Sign in with your phone number or email address

Add your drivers

  1. 1Tap Account
  2. 2Tap Manage Contract Drivers
  3. 3Tap + Add
  4. 4Fill in the driver details → Tap Create
⚠️
Important

Check Send SMS invite so the driver gets a text. Add all your drivers here — both internal and contracted haulers.

Add dispatchers and admins

  1. 1Tap Account
  2. 2Tap Manage Users
  3. 3Enter the user's name, email, and phone
  4. 4Check the boxes to send an email or SMS invite
  5. 5Pick the right role for the user
  6. 6Tap Submit to create the user
Chapter 3 · Mobile Dispatch

Accept and assign jobs on mobile.

From accepting a customer's job to sending it to a driver — all from your phone.

Mobile · Dispatch

Accept jobs and assign drivers

Accept a customer's jobs, assign them to your drivers, and send them out — in seconds from your phone.

Accept jobs from your customer

  1. 1Tap Accept All to take every pending job at once
  2. 2Or tap the down arrow ▼ next to the truck count to accept individually
  3. 3Accepted jobs show an Accepted status and are ready to assign

Open the assignment view

  1. 1Tap the down arrow ▼ next to the order truck count to see assigned trucks
  2. 2Tap + Assign to start adding a driver

Pick a driver and send

  1. 1Search for a driver by name or truck number
  2. 2Tap Send to push the job to the driver
📱
What happens next

The driver gets a push notification. They can accept and start the job right from their phone.

Chapter 4 · Web Platform

Set up from your desk.

Same setup, more room to work. Add trucks, drivers, and users from any browser.

Web · Vendor Admin

Full account setup on the web platform

Log into the Tread web platform at app.tread-horizon.com and get your full account configured.

Log into the Tread web platform

  1. 1Go to app.tread-horizon.com
  2. 2Enter your email or phone number
  3. 3Enter your password or login code

Add your trucks

  1. 1Click Settings
  2. 2Click Equipment
  3. 3Click Create Equipment
  4. 4Add the truck number or name and pick an equipment type → Click Create

Add and invite your drivers

  1. 1Click Settings
  2. 2Click Drivers
  3. 3Click Create Driver
  4. 4Fill in the driver's details
⚠️
Important

Check Send SMS Invitation so the driver gets a text to download the app.

Assign default trucks to drivers

If your drivers typically drive the same truck, pair them once and Tread carries it through.

  1. 1Click Settings
  2. 2Click Drivers
  3. 3Click into the Equipment box on each driver row and pick their truck
💡
Why this helps

The truck name shows up alongside the driver every time you dispatch them. Fewer clicks, fewer mistakes.

Add dispatchers and admins

  1. 1Click Settings
  2. 2Click Users
  3. 3Click Create User
  4. 4Pick the right Role for the user (dispatcher, admin, etc.)
Chapter 5 · Web Dispatch

Run dispatch at scale.

Accept jobs, assign drivers, send them out. From browser to driver's phone in seconds.

Web · Dispatch

Accept, assign, and send jobs from the browser

The web platform gives you full visibility and control over every dispatch — right from your desk.

Accept jobs from your customer

  1. 1Click Accept on each truck row to take jobs one at a time
  2. 2Or click Accept All at the top of the order to take every pending row at once

Assign drivers to jobs

  1. 1Click Assign on the job row — a panel opens with Driver and Vendor tabs
  2. 2In the Driver tab, search and pick one of your own drivers
  3. 3In the Vendor tab, pass the job to a multi-truck hauler who'll sub-dispatch

Send jobs out to drivers

When you're ready to dispatch, drivers get the notification on their phone.

  1. 1Click Send on a single row to dispatch that driver
  2. 2Or click Send All Jobs at the top to push every pending assignment at once
⚡
Heads up

Send All Jobs dispatches every assignment still in pending status. Double-check the list before clicking.
